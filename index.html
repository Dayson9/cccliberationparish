About Church

Celestial Church of Christ Liberation Parish is a Church that is founded by Ven/Sup/Evang.J.G Aso through our great Father Rev/Pst/Founder S.B.J Oshoffa.

The Church resides at the far edge of Nigeria(Lagos State) which is known for it's diverse abundance of water bodies.

CCC Liberation Parish is currently being led by our father Ven/Sup/Evang.J.G Aso and Assistant Shepherd Bankole.
